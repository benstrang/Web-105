Star Wars Battlefront 2

Launch

Star Wars Battlefront 2 was a highly anticipated release. The last "good" star wars game was the Star Wars Battlefront 2 from the year 2005, this was released on the PS2 so we've been waiting awhile for a good star wars game to be released again.The launch of the game was very successful. The game was alive for a couple months before slowly dying off as EA added in a loot crate system making the game purely pay to win. As of recently, the game has been completely rethought, loot crates removed; new heroes and villains added; and as far as player base goes, they've passed up their initial launch numbers. Which is incredible for a game thats 3 years old.

There was a remake of the original battlefront but it just didn't live up to the hype. Battlefront 2, however, has definitely captured the Star Wars feel that we've all been waiting for.

Story Mode

"Told over 13 chapters, the story follows Iden Versio, commander of the Empire’s elite Inferno Squad, and starts with her just moments before the climax of Return of the Jedi. She’s on a mission to destroy evidence that would warn the Rebels about the Empire’s secret attack near Endor, and after pulling that off she’s sent to run a mission on the forest moon — where she sees the second Death Star blown apart. Her father, an Imperial Admiral, brings her in to help initiate the last directive from The Emperor — something called Operation: Cinder," click for source here. I'll try to not spoil the story incase one of you decides to pick up the game, but it brings back the same nostalgia from our favorite heroes and villians in the star wars galaxy and also adds some detail to back stories of some heroes.

Multiplayer

The multiplayer for battlefront follows the same theme as the previous games but with the added features and modes. The three most popular game modes include Walker Assualt; Supremacy; amd Turning Point. All of these modes are 40 vs 40 and take place on your favorite Star Wars planets. There's also a heroes vs villains mode, this mode is 4 vs 4. One side will be villains and one side will be heroes. A typical line up for this gamemode would be Darth Vader, Darth Maul, Palpatine, and Kylo Ren for the villains, and then for the Heroes, Luke, Anakin, Obi Wan, and Han. You aren't limited to this these four, there's around 10 characters for each side to choose from. There's also a very similar mode, except you are picking from the heroes or villains fighter pilots. Then you fly around the place trying to shoot each other down.
